Toll Gate Blood Drive

Toll Gate High School runs multiple blood drives a year. It is open to all students and staff, usually from 8:00 am to 2:00 pm. The blood drive is usually operated by a teacher and a student representative. Students 16 or older are encouraged to talk to a parent/guardian about donating. These blood donations can help out so many people.

    Donation Requirements
  • You have not donated in the past 2 months
  • You are in good health
  • At least 16 years old (if 16, you must have a signed permission slip)
  • Must weigh at least 110 pounds
